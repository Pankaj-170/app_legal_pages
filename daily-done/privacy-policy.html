Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Done ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Our Commitment

Daily Done is designed to work completely offline. The app's core functionality — creating tasks, tracking daily progress, viewing calendars and stats — runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the data you enter into the app. Your tasks, entries, and usage records remain on your device at all times.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. We do not require an account, registration, or login. We do not access your contacts, photos, location, or any other personal data stored on your device.

Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device advertising ID (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system version
  • General location (not precise GPS)
  • Ad interactions (views, taps)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not receive any personally identifiable information from AdMob. AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy and is beyond our control.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no information for us to use, share, or sell. The sole purpose of any automated data collection (by AdMob) is to serve advertisements within the App.

Data Storage and Security

All app data — including tasks, entries, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Realm, an embedded mobile database. No data is transmitted to external servers. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the app's local storage through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California), you may have rights regarding your data. Since we do not collect or store any personal data, these rights are inherently respected. For data collected by AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy to exercise your rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via GitHub Issues at github.com/anomalyco/dailytaskchecker.
