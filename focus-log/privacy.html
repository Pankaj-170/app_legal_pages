Privacy Policy

App: Focus Log
Last Updated: April 21, 2026

Focus Log ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Focus Log mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Focus Log, we may collect the following information:

  • Name or profile information you choose to provide
  • Phone number or Google account information used for login
  • Focus sessions, task lists, goals, streaks, and productivity history
  • App settings, theme preferences, and gamification progress
  • Device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports to improve app performance

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save your focus sessions, tasks, and progress
  • Provide reminders, streak tracking, and personalized recommendations
  • Improve the app experience and fix bugs
  • Enable premium features, rewards, and in-app purchases
  • Send important service-related notifications

3. Authentication

If you sign in using Google or your phone number, authentication is securely handled by Firebase Authentication or other third-party authentication providers. We do not store your password.

4. Data Storage

Your data may be stored securely using cloud services such as Firebase or Supabase. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Analytics and Advertising

Focus Log may use analytics tools such as Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app.

The app may also display optional rewarded advertisements. If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, advertising partners may collect limited device information to deliver and measure the ad.

6. In-App Purchases

Focus Log may offer premium subscriptions or one-time purchases. Payments are processed securely through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only with:

  • Authentication providers
  • Cloud storage and analytics providers
  • Advertising providers for rewarded ads
  • Payment providers for subscriptions and purchases
  • Authorities if required by law

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within a reasonable period unless we are required to keep certain information by law.

9. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access the information we store about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Disable notifications or analytics from your device settings

10. Children's Privacy

Focus Log is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will notify you through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page.
