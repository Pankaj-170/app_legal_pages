1. Who we are

TradeVision is a personal trading journal that stores your trading data on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, why, and with whom (if anyone) it is shared. For the terms governing use of the App, see our Terms and Conditions.

2. What data we collect

The App processes the following data that you provide or that is generated as you use it:

  • Trade records: asset/market, direction, entry & exit prices, position size, fees, timestamps, status.
  • Imported exchange data: order history, trade history, and balances fetched from exchanges you connect (currently CoinDCX and Binance).
  • Self-reported context: strategy, timeframe, session, emotion/mood, notes, and tags.
  • Trade images: screenshots you attach, and any text extracted from them on your device.
  • Exchange credentials: API key and secret you enter to enable import (stored only on your device — see Section 8).
  • Device preferences: appearance (light/dark), and a flag recording that you enabled device biometric unlock. We never receive your biometric information.
  • Usage analytics (optional): aggregated, non-identifiable interaction events used solely to improve the App. This is disabled by default where applicable and never includes your trading values.

3. Where it comes from

  • Entered directly by you (manual trade logging, notes, images).
  • Fetched from the exchange you connect, using the API credentials you supply.
  • Generated locally by the App (analytics, auto-derived strategies, insights, discipline scoring, experiments).

4. How we use your data

  • To display your journal, P&L, equity curve, and performance analytics.
  • To import and match your exchange orders into round-trip trades.
  • To derive strategies, insights, discipline scores, and experiments.
  • To apply your chosen appearance and lock the App with your device biometrics.
  • To improve the App through optional, aggregated analytics.

5. Legal basis

We process the above on the basis of your consent (given when you install the App, connect an exchange, enable analytics, or use a feature) and our legitimate interest in operating and improving the Service. You may withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use or clearing the App's local data.

6. Data sharing & disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data, and we do not operate advertising SDKs that profile you. Your data is shared only in these limited cases:

  • With your exchange: when you connect an account, your API credentials are sent directly to that exchange's own servers over HTTPS so the App can fetch your orders, trade history, and balances. This is initiated by you and is between your device and the exchange.
  • With a custom sync server (optional): if you configure a custom sync endpoint, your data is transmitted to the server you specify, under your own configuration. We are not responsible for that server's practices.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose data where required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property.

7. Biometric data

If you enable app-lock, the App uses your device's built-in biometric authentication (Face ID or fingerprint) through the operating system's secure APIs. Biometric data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. The App stores only a flag indicating that biometric unlock is enabled; the actual biometric match is performed by the device OS.

8. Storage & security

  • On-device database: trade records, analytics, and configuration are stored in a local SQLite database on your phone.
  • On-device key-value storage: preferences and cached state use on-device storage (e.g., MMKV).
  • Secure credential store: exchange API keys/secrets are kept in the platform secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) and are accessed only to call your exchange.
  • Transport security: any communication with an exchange or a custom sync server uses HTTPS/TLS.

No trading data is transmitted to us. The security of any connected exchange or custom sync server is outside our control.

9. Retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, it is retained until you remove it. You can delete individual trades in the App, or remove everything via Settings → Clear All Data. Disconnecting an exchange removes its stored credentials from your secure credential store. Uninstalling the App removes its local data from your device.

10. Children

TradeVision is not directed to and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Because your data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights directly within the App (view, edit, export, or clear) without needing to contact us. For any assistance, email pankajsoni1705@gmail.com.
