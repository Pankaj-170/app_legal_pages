2. The Service

TradeVision is a personal trading journal. It helps you record, organise, and analyse your own trading activity. The App lets you:

  • Manually log trades (entry/exit price, direction, size, fees, notes, tags, emotions).
  • Import trade history directly from connected exchanges (currently CoinDCX and Binance) using your own API credentials.
  • Automatically match buy and sell orders into round-trip trades using a first-in-first-out (FIFO) model.
  • View analytics such as cumulative P&L (equity curve), weekly and monthly P&L, win rate, profit factor, expectancy, holding/fee/spacing discipline, and per-asset performance.
  • Build automatic "strategies" grouped by market, plus insights, a personal rulebook, psychology/emotion tracking, and simple A/B trading experiments.
  • Attach trade screenshots, which are processed on your device to extract details.
  • Lock the App on launch with your device biometrics (Face ID / fingerprint).

3. Accounts & Exchange Connections

The App does not require you to create a hosted account with us. To import trades you connect a third-party exchange by providing an API key and secret. You should create read-only API keys on the exchange and never grant withdrawal rights. We transmit your credentials only to the relevant exchange's own servers (over HTTPS) to fetch orders, trade history, and balances on your request. We store exchange credentials only in your device's secure credential store (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) and we never transmit them to us.

If you configure an optional custom sync server, your data is transmitted to the endpoint you specify, in accordance with your own configuration. We are not responsible for the security or practices of any third-party exchange or custom server you choose to connect.

4. Features & Data You Provide

All trade data, notes, tags, emotions, images, and configuration are stored locally on your device (SQLite database and on-device key-value storage). The data you enter or import may include:

  • Trade details: asset/market, direction, entry & exit prices, position size, fees, timestamps, status.
  • Exchange-imported order history, balances, and trade history fetched via your API keys.
  • Self-reported context: strategy, timeframe, session, emotion, notes, and tags.
  • Trade screenshots and any text extracted from them on-device.
  • Device biometric unlock preference (we store only a flag; we never receive your biometric data).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal trading data with advertisers. Aggregated, non-identifiable usage analytics (if enabled) may be used solely to improve the App.
